"The Devastating Impact of Drugs"

Drug abuse has far-reaching consequences that impact not just the individual but also families and society as a whole. It deteriorates physical health, leading to life-threatening conditions such as heart disease, liver failure, and weakened immunity. Mentally, it triggers anxiety, depression, and cognitive impairment, making it difficult to lead a stable life. Socially, drug abuse strains relationships, causes financial distress, and often leads to legal troubles.
